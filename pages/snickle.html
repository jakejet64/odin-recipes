The Snickle

Crispy, Juicy, Snickle

Ingredients
  • Snickers Bar
  • Appropiately sized Pickle
Directions
  1. Cut pickle in half long-wise
  2. Hollow out both halves of pickle to accomodate a Snickers Bar
  3. Place Snickers Bar into a half, and place other half back on top
  4. Take a bite and experience pure ecstasy
